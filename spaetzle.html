Spätzle

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 large eggs
  • ½ cup whole milk
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp butter

Instructions

  1. Whisk flour, eggs, milk, and salt into a sticky batter.
  2. Press batter through a spätzle maker (or colander) into boiling salted water.
  3. Cook until noodles float (about 2 minutes). Remove and drain.
  4. Sauté lightly in butter before serving.
