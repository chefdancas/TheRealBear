Twice-Fried Beef Tallow French Fries

Ingredients

  • 4 large russet potatoes
  • Beef tallow for frying
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Peel and cut potatoes into fries. Soak in cold water for 30 minutes, then drain and pat dry.
  2. Heat beef tallow to 325°F. Fry potatoes in batches until soft but not brown (about 4 minutes). Remove and drain.
  3. Increase tallow heat to 375°F. Fry potatoes again until golden and crispy.
  4. Drain on paper towels, season with salt, and serve hot.
