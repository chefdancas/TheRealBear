Twice-Fried Beef Tallow Fries

Classic double-cook for glassy crunch and creamy centers.

Story

Add your fry-station lore—oil management, blanch timing, and why tallow changes the game.

Ingredients

  • 4 large russet potatoes
  • Beef tallow (enough for deep frying)
  • Kosher salt

Method

  1. Cut fries, soak 30 min, rinse, and dry thoroughly.
  2. Blanch at 325°F until tender but pale (about 4–5 min). Drain and cool.
  3. Final fry at 375°F until deeply golden and crisp. Season immediately.
