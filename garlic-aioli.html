Garlic Aioli

Ingredients

  • 2 egg yolks
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 cup olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Whisk egg yolks, garlic, and mustard until smooth.
  2. Slowly drizzle in olive oil while whisking to emulsify.
  3. Add lemon juice and salt. Mix until creamy.
  4. Chill and serve as a dip or spread.
