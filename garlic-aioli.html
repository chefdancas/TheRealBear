Garlic Aioli

Classic emulsion—silky, punchy, perfect with fries or grilled veg.

Story

Tell the story behind your aioli here (service hacks, menu context, your technique philosophy, etc.).

Ingredients

  • 2 egg yolks
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely grated
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 cup neutral oil or olive oil (or split)
  • 1–2 Tbsp lemon juice
  • Kosher salt

Method

  1. Whisk yolks, garlic, and Dijon until smooth.
  2. Very slowly stream in oil while whisking to emulsify.
  3. Balance with lemon and salt. Thin with a few drops of water if needed.
